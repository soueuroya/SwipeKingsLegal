Privacy Policy for SwipeKings

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Boldreams ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you play our mobile game, SwipeKings (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or log in to SwipeKings, we collect certain personal information via our backend provider, Supabase:

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and recovery.
  • User ID: A unique identifier associated with your account.
  • Nickname/Username: If provided, to identify you on leaderboards.

1.2 Game Data

We collect data related to your gameplay to provide game features:

  • Scores and Progress: We save your high scores, badge progression, and game mode statistics.
  • Play Limits: We track your daily play counts for specific game modes to enforce fair usage limits.

1.3 Technical and Analytics Data

We use Unity Analytics and other third-party services to collect information about how the App is used:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Time spent in the App, features accessed, and game session details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: Authenticating users and saving game progress.
  • Leaderboards: Displaying your scores and nickname to other players.
  • Personalization: Improving your game experience.
  • Analytics: Understanding how players interact with the game to provide updates and improvements.
  • Compliance: Enforcing our terms and daily play limits.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that facilitate our service (e.g., Supabase for backend services, Unity for game engine and analytics).
  • Google Play Services: If you use Google Play Games features, your data may be shared with Google according to their privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and Update: You can update your profile information within the App.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard security measures through our service providers (Supabase/Google) to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

SwipeKings is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
